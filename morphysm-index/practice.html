BLACK BOOK

Clinical version of "The Burning." Morphystic use: administer prior to intentional memory/code activation. Without activation, effect is minimal. Beta-blockers act as cooling agents in the Burning, tempering intensity so self-model's code can be safely examined and rewritten.
